Detailed Course Information

Unlock your artistic potential with our Beginner's Watercolor Course!

Whether you're an aspiring artist or simply eager to explore your creative side, our course provides a supportive and enriching environment to nurture your artistic talents. Join us on a captivating journey into the world of watercolours.

This course is open to complete beginners - no prior experience needed!

What You'll Learn:

  • Techniques: Master essential watercolor techniques, such as wet-on-wet, wet-on-dry, layering, and negative painting.
  • Composition: Understand the golden ratio, foreground-middleground-background, and perspective to add depth to your artwork.
  • Colour Theory: Explore the world of colours and learn to create vibrant and nuanced imagery by mixing paints on the paper.
  • Motif Variety: Each pair of lessons offers fresh perspectives, from still life objects to dynamic seascapes and more, allowing you to expand your creative horizon.

What You'll Create

  1. Lessons 1-2: Still Life - Paint flowers, fruits, pots, bottles, rusty objects, stones, and shells
  2. Lessons 3-4: Water World - Dive into seascapes and lakes, featuring boats, fishes, and seabirds
  3. Lessons 5-6: Times Gone By & Faraway Places - Capture the charm of old buildings, streets, clothing, tools
  4. Lessons 7-8: Human Portraits - Master portraits, bodies, and movement
  5. Lessons 9-10: Animal Artistry - Choose your favourite animal, even your pet, for a delightful finishing touch
